Our Story

Who We Are

The Parkland Female U15 AAA Rangers are a brand new elite female hockey program built for and by the Parkland Region. For too long, elite-level female players in our communities have faced an impossible choice: relocate to Winnipeg, billet away from family, or play below their potential. The Rangers exist to change that.

We are a Hockey Manitoba sanctioned U15 AAA program competing in the Rural Manitoba U15 AAA Female Hockey League. Our team draws players from across the Parkland — young women who deserve access to elite coaching, structured development, and competitive hockey without leaving home.

A Message to Parkland Families

It has been determined that there is enough interest in the Parkland Region to pursue the formation of the Parkland Rangers Female U15 AAA Program for the upcoming 2025–2026 season. We are excited about what this means for female hockey in our communities.

We want your daughter to be part of this program.

This is the female hockey option for players in the Parkland Region. Please be aware that only players who register with the Parkland Rangers Female U15 AAA Program are eligible for a release to other programs. We encourage all eligible players to register and be part of something special right here at home.
